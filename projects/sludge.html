Sludge

Information

Mobile multiplayer game made with Unity and C#. Published to Google Play. Worked on the project in an On-the-job training. The On-the-job training lasted for 3 months Worked on refactoring movement to be run on server to prevent cheating. Created the power-up system as a skills demonstration.

Gameplay

Players can pick different characters. players move by jumping around while trying to land on other players to score points. There are power-ups and downs that can be collected to give the players an advantage.
