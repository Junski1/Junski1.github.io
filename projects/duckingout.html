Ducking Out

Information

A endless runner mobile game made with Unity and C# made for android. Made in school during Information and communication technology upper secondary qualification degree. Published to Project done in a group of 3 students for around 2 months. Worked as a programmer responsible for gameplay, score system, save and replay system and UI functionality.

Gameplay

Objective of the game is to fly in the sky as a duck and eat prawns that jump from the water while avoiding hitting the water. Salmons jump out of the water trying to knock the duck out of the sky. While eagles prey on the sky trying to eat the duck.
