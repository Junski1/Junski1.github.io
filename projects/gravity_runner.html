Gravity Runner

Information

Gravity Runner is a hyper casual endless runner made with Unity and C# for PC and mobile. This project was made as a task for a course. It is Published to Itch.io. The objective of the game is to survive for as long as possible.

Gameplay

The player has a single input. They can switch their gravity. The player has to survive by avoiding spikes and falling up or down.
