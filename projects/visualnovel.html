Visual Novel

Information

A Visual novel made with Unity and C#. A solo project made in school during Information and communication technology upper secondary qualification degree as a skills demonstration. Has dialogue and minigames. Story is read from a text file.

Gameplay

Player plays as Pac-Man and meets with Red. Player moves through the story making choises. The game ends with a minigame chosen by the player. One of them being a penalty shootout with Red. The game randomly selects the first penalty taker. The 2 characters goes on goal in turns. The goal has 6 possible shooting targets. The story has different ending depending on the choises the player makes and how the player does in the minigame.
